Tailor-Made Travels

Our company organizes travels that are fulfilling 100% of your needs and exceptions. If you wish to learn to know about Chinese, Korean or Japanese tea culture, we can plan a tour where you are going to learn all the aspects about tea, how it grows, how it is produced and how it is sold. You will also learn how the tea should be served and what you are supposed to eat and do while enjoying your tea.

If you are more interested about handicrafts in East Asia, we will take you to see for example Japanese carpenters. Or if you rather know more about Chinese medicine or learn about Japanese bathing culture, well, look no further. We will arrange you a trip where you will learn about those.

All our guides speak perfect English and the local language of your destination. They will act as your guides and translators. They are perfectly capable to handle also all the emergency situations and can help you in case you need to see a doctor.

Accommodation is organized according to your wishes. Flights and other travel will also be arranged as you wish. If you wish to have luxury, that is what you will get. If you would rather experience simple life, then that is what you get.

Our priority is to provide you exiting experiences safely. Thus, it might happen that in some occasions our guide will suggest some other solution to your wishes. For example, if you wish to learn to know street food in East Asia, we might occasionally suggest you a different location, just to be safe that you will complete your travel in good health.
